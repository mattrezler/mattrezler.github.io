Matt (Mateusz) Rezler, PharmD, PhD

Cheminformatics Specialist at Merck Group

I am a scientist dealing with topics on the borderline of chemistry, biology and computer science. In the past I have been involved in the design and synthesis of drug-like compounds and epigenetic analyses. My current research focuses on cheminformatics and machine learning solutions in retrosynthesis. I am developing towards other applications of artificial intelligence with commercial applications along with improving full stack web developer skills.
